Privacy Policy for DogeMask Chrome Extension

Last updated: April 30th 2023

Welcome to the DogeMask Chrome Extension (the "Extension") developed by the DogeMask Development Team ("us," "we," or "our"). Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information while using our Extension. By installing, accessing, or using the Extension, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

While using our Extension, we may collect the following types of information:

  • a. Dogecoin Addresses: We store your Dogecoin addresses on our servers to associate them with your Twitter handle. We do not store your private keys.
  • b. Private Keys: Your private keys are stored in your browser's local storage, and we do not have access to them.
  • c. Twitter Handle: We collect your Twitter handle to associate them with your Dogecoin Address..
  • d. Cookies: We use cookies from "auth.dogemask.lol" to authenticate and maintain the logged-in status of users.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • a. To provide, maintain, and improve the Extension and its functionality.
  • b. To authenticate users and maintain their logged-in status for a seamless experience.
  • c. To facilitate integration with your Twitter account and associate your Dogecoin addresses with your Twitter handle.

3. Storage and Security

The security of your personal information is important to us, and we take reasonable measures to protect it. Your Dogecoin addresses are stored on our servers, while your private keys are stored in your browser's local storage. We do not have custody of your tokens or private keys.

However, please be aware that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We strongly recommend that you maintain a backup of your important information.

4. Cookies

Cookies are small files that are placed on your computer, mobile device, or any other device by a website containing the details of your browsing history on that website. We use cookies from "auth.dogemask.lol" to authenticate and maintain the logged-in status of users. You can modify your browser settings to block or delete cookies, but this may affect the functionality of our Extension.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the "Last updated" date at the top of the policy. By continuing to access or use our Extension after changes become effective, you agree to be bound by the revised Privacy Policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at mason@kissr.co.

By using the DogeMask Chrome Extension, you acknowledge and agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
